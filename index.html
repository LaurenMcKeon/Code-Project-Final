About Us

We are a building façade restoration company with over 20 years experience in the conservation and restoration of listed buildings, protected structures and national monuments.

Summit have had the privilege of working on some of Ireland’s finest buildings . We were very proud & delighted when our work at Trinity College was featured on RTE News on 7th March 2013 Our portfolio of work is broad ranging from the original Parliament Building (now Bank of Ireland) on College Green, to the Royal College of Physicians of Ireland, Killeen Castle and Martello towers to modest Victorian bungalows, all restored and conserved with the utmost of care, craftsmanship and pride. Our client base includes, Local Authorities, Universities, Schools, Property Management Companies as well as numerous building contractors and private building owners.
